## Introduction to AI and ML

Artificial Intelligence (AI) refers to the broader field of research and development aimed at creating machines that can perform tasks that typically require human intelligence. Machine Learning (ML) is a subset of AI that involves the development of algorithms and statistical models that enable computers to learn and improve their performance on a task without being explicitly programmed.

## Deep Learning: A Subfield of ML

Deep Learning (DL) is a subfield of ML that focuses on learning hierarchical representations of data using neural networks with multiple layers. This type of learning enables machines to learn complex patterns and relationships in data, making it a crucial component of artificial intelligence.

## Data Science: The Backbone of AI and ML

Data Science is a multidisciplinary field that encompasses a range of techniques and tools, including ML, DL, and programming. Data Science involves the analysis, interpretation, and visualization of complex data sets, providing insights that inform AI model development and improvement. By mastering data science concepts, such as data cleaning, preprocessing, and visualization, developers and researchers can unlock insights that drive innovation and business growth.
